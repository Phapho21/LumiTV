C_SCROLLBAR
Definition
Scroll bar allows user to recognize the direction and the range of lists/contents.
Usage
  • - The scroll bar is shown on the screen only when all the content cannot be displayed on the same page.
  • - It is displayed on the right or at the bottom of the scrolling area.
  • - The method of displaying the scroll bar can vary depending on the display area (a list or a text area).
Interaction
Specs
Properties
A scroll bar consists of the following elements:
1. Track (Mandatory)
2. Thumb (Mandatory)
Length
  • - The lengths of the thumb and track are proportional to the current position and the total number.
