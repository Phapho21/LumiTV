Definition
Paring is used to let users know the connection status between the TV and other devices.
This informs users of whether a connection is in progress, successful, or failed.
Usage
  • - Use this to let user know the connection status between the TV and other devices.
  • - Make it displayed until the connection is completed and place it at the top center of the screen.
  • - Provide the text that a brief message while connecting and after the connection is complete.
  • - Provide the text that a brief message after the connection failed and users are not required to take any further actions.
  • - Example: Remote Control Pairing, etc.
Specs
Properties
Pairing consists of the following elements :
1. Icon/Image (Mandatory)
2. Text (Optional)
