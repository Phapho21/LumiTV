C_DATETIMEINFO
Definition
Date & Time Information is used to present time and date information on screen.
Usage
  • - Follow the standard date format used in each respective country.
  • - Display dates with two or more elements of year, month, date, and day of the week.
  • - Display times in a 24-hour or 12-hour format.
  • - When presenting both the time and date on one screen, place the date before the time.
  • - Each guide conforms to the American date format.
Specs
Properties
Date & Time Information consists of the following elements:
1. Year (Optional)
2. Month (Optional)
3. Date (Optional)
4. Day of the week (Optional)
5. Time (Optional)
  • - In case of 12-hour format, display time with am/pm or 오전/오후 in each respective country.
