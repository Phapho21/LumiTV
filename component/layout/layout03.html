Layout 03

  • Definition
  • Specs
  • Examples
Definition
Use Layout 03 to display groupings of related items, such as genres of movies or TV shows. View the list of groupings and focus on one to see its items.
If the primary distinguishing content consists of images, use Layout 03.
Layout 03 is best suited to presenting homogenous.
Specs
Properties
  • Detail view
  • Simple view
Note
  • 1. Display the title that represents the current screen. In general, the title can be the name of an application, a device, and etc.
  • 2. Grids of items are scrolled vertically.
  • 3. Image items used in Layout 03 should include a main image.
  • 4. Each group of related items can have a title.
  • 5. When the focus moves up and down, display the vertical scroll bar on the side of the screen.
  • 6. Buttons to sort or to filter the items in a grid are located just above the grid.
  • 7. Buttons for functions that will affect to a grid are located just above the grid, such as Edit.
  • 8. Tabs enable content organization at a high level. Tab labels should succinctly describe the content within.
  • 9. Buttons for functions that will affect to the whole screen can be located on this area.
    They should be composed of only a text.
Initial State
  • - The first tab is selected.
  • - The focus is on the first(top-left) item of the grid in the first(top) tab.
