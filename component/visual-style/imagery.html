1. Realistic Image

For representing actual products and devices, the images should represent as realistic as possible. These should be made clear with photo-realism techniques and state-of-art representations.

Abstract expressions and movements such as arrows, wavelengths, etc. should be represented as 2D Graphic.

3. Preview
